---
title: Build your first workflow
description: Author a contract-review workflow on the visual canvas, step by step, in about 15 minutes.
---

By the end of this walkthrough you'll have a three-state workflow with an SLA, a two-person approval chain, and an automation that posts to Slack on breach. Every step is doable on the canvas — we'll also show the YAML so you can see what the canvas is writing.

<Callout>
Prefer YAML-first? The equivalent workflow file is at the bottom of this page. You can paste it into `.kiket/workflows/contract_review.yaml` in your workspace repo and skip the canvas.
</Callout>

## Goal: a contract-review workflow

You need legal to review every contract, CFO to co-sign anything over $100k, and the team to be paged if review drags past 48 hours.

<Steps>

<Step>

### Open the visual editor

From your project's sidebar, click **Workflows → New workflow**. Enter a key (`contract_review`), a name (`Contract Review`), and click **Create**.

You'll land in the visual editor with an empty canvas and a single **Intake** state pre-dropped.

</Step>

<Step>

### Add the Legal Review state

Click the **+ Add state** button in the toolbar. Name it **Legal Review**. The canvas places it to the right of Intake; drag it wherever you like.

Click the state to open its inspector. In the **Category** dropdown, leave it as *Active*.

</Step>

<Step>

### Connect Intake → Legal Review

Hover Intake; a small arrow appears on its right edge. Drag from the arrow to the Legal Review state. A transition is created. Click the transition itself to name it — `Start review`.

</Step>

<Step>

### Add the SLA

Back in the inspector for Legal Review, scroll to **SLA**. Set:

- **Warning**: `24h` (notify team early)
- **Breach**: `48h` (escalate to on-call)

Click **Save**. The canvas commits the change; you'll see a small green dot in the top-right for a second.

<Callout title="What just happened?">
The canvas wrote a YAML edit to `.kiket/workflows/contract_review.yaml` and committed it to your workspace repo with a message like "Update Legal Review SLA". Open the repo on GitHub to see it.
</Callout>

</Step>

<Step>

### Add the approval chain

Still in the Legal Review inspector, scroll to **Approval**. Click **Add approver**. Pick `counsel` (the counsel group in your org). Add another approver: `cfo`. Leave the type as *Sequential*.

Now *any* transition out of Legal Review requires counsel → CFO sign-off in order.

</Step>

<Step>

### Add the Approved state

Click **+ Add state**. Name it **Approved**. In the inspector, set **Category** to *Final*. Drag a transition from Legal Review to Approved and name it `Approve`.

</Step>

<Step>

### Wire the breach automation

On the Legal Review state, open the inspector's **Automations** tab. Click **Add automation** and pick the trigger `on_sla_breach`. For the action, pick **Send notification** and choose your Slack channel from the dropdown (if you haven't installed Slack yet, do that first — see [Your first extension](/docs/start/first-extension)).

Save. Now when an issue sits in Legal Review past 48h, Slack gets a message.

</Step>

<Step>

### Test it

Click **Simulate** in the top-right. The simulator drops a dummy issue into Intake and walks it through transitions. You'll see:

- Green check when the transition is allowed.
- Red cross + reason when it's blocked (missing approval, SLA not expired, etc.).

Simulate is read-only; it doesn't create real issues.

</Step>

<Step>

### Ship it

Click **Publish**. The workflow becomes available on the project's boards. Anyone can now create an issue against it.

Your repo's `.kiket/workflows/contract_review.yaml` now contains the result of everything you did on the canvas.

</Step>

</Steps>

## The YAML the canvas wrote

```yaml
key: contract_review
name: Contract Review
states:
  intake:
    category: initial

  legal_review:
    category: active
    sla:
      warning: 24h
      breach: 48h
    approval:
      type: sequential
      chain: [counsel, cfo]
    on_sla_breach:
      - notify:
          channel: slack:#contracts

  approved:
    category: final

transitions:
  - from: intake
    to: legal_review
    name: Start review
  - from: legal_review
    to: approved
    name: Approve
    approval_required: true
```
